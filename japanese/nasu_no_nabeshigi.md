Nasu no Nabeshigi - Pork, eggplant and paprika in miso sauce
============================================================

Ingredients
-----------

* 2 eggplants
* 2 green paprikas (aka bell peppers)
* 150g of pork fillet

Sauce:
* 5 tbsp miso paste
* 3 tbsp sugar
* 1 tbsp mirin
* 1 tbsp water
* 1 tsp soy sauce

Cut paprika and eggplant to 1 cm thick slices.  Slice pork meat to very thin slices, and the cut the slices to about 1cm wide.  Brown the meat,
then add eggplant.  When it's about half done, add paprika, and when it's almost done, add the sauce.
